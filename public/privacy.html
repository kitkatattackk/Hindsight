Privacy Policy — Last updated April 14, 2026

✦ The short version

Hindsight stores everything on your device. We don't collect your data, don't have accounts, don't run analytics, and don't talk to any servers. Your reflections are yours alone.

📦 What data we collect

Hindsight collects no personal data. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any identifying information.

All journal entries, mood scores, regret logs, and settings are stored exclusively in your device's local storage and never leave your device.

⚡ How data is used

Data you enter into Hindsight is used only to power the app's features on your device:

  • Displaying your journal and reflection history
  • Generating dashboard insights and pattern recognition
  • Calculating streaks and awarding wisdom badges
  • Scheduling local push notifications (if enabled)

🔒 Third-party sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to any third party — ever. There are no advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or crash-reporting services embedded in the app.

🔔 Notifications

If you enable nightly reminders, Hindsight uses your device's local notification system. Notification preferences are stored locally. We do not use any push notification service that sends data to external servers.

👶 Children's privacy

Hindsight is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because no data leaves your device, there is no risk of children's data being transmitted to us.

📝 Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy (for example, if we add optional cloud sync in the future), we will update the date at the top of this page and notify users within the app before any changes take effect.
