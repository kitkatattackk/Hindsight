## Full Description

Ever wish you could see your own patterns before they become habits?

Hindsight is a nightly reflection tool that helps you log the decisions weighing on you, track your emotional patterns over time, and turn regret into wisdom — one ritual at a time.

**Your Nightly Ritual**
Each evening, Molly — your wise and empathetic companion — guides you through a quick check-in. Rate your day, log what's weighing on you, and tag each decision by category. It takes less than 2 minutes.

**See Your Patterns**
The dashboard reveals what your journal can't — visual insights that show you where your regrets cluster, which days hit hardest, and how your mood connects to the decisions you make.

- 📉 Regret Decay Chart — watch your average regret trend over 30 days
- 📊 Category Breakdown — see which areas of life carry the most emotional weight
- 🗓️ Weekly Digest — a narrative summary of every week with Molly's take
- 🧠 Pattern Recognition — your toughest day, top regret category, and mood correlations
- 🏅 Wisdom Badges — earn achievements as you build self-awareness

**Your Data, Your Device**
Hindsight is 100% private. Everything is stored locally on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data collection — ever.

**Built for reflection, not obsession**
Hindsight isn't a mood tracker you open 10 times a day. It's a quiet, intentional ritual designed to help you slow down, reflect, and make better decisions tomorrow.
